GAME

The game screen is a maze, the corridors of which are filled with dots. The player's task is to control Pacman to eat all the points in the maze, avoiding meeting the ghosts that are chasing the hero. At the beginning of each level, the ghosts are located in a rectangular room in the middle of the level inaccessible to Pacman, from which they are eventually freed. If the ghost touches Pacman, then his life is lost, the ghosts and Pacman return to their original position, but the progress of the collected points is saved. If Pacman has no extra lives left after colliding with a ghost, the game ends. After removing all the points, a new level begins in the same maze. On the sides of the maze there are two entrances to one tunnel, upon entering which Pacman and the ghosts exit from the other side of the maze.
